Bras Ouverts – Open Arms

The main goal of Bras Ouverts – Open Arms (BOOA) is to establish a sustainably financed school that provides free education in Ouidah, Benin. BOOA serves a community of Nigerian refugees who fled environmental destruction, as well as marginalized Beninese children. In particular, we prioritize the education and empowerment of women and girls.
