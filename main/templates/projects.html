Projects & Collaboration

Education

  • BOOA runs an educational center called Peace Academy for children in need of assistance. The center provides instruction in English and French.

Pedagogical Development

  • Our teachers continue to further their own educations through online courses and consultation with experts.

Library – Computer Lab

  • We are gathering resources to assemble a library – lab that will provide regular access to reading materials and information technologies.

Legal Aid

  • We are assisting with the procurement of legal identification documents and residence permits for displaced persons Benin.
  • We are also aiding families with obtaining birth certificates for unregistered children.

Access to Clean Water

  • In collaboration with Northwestern University's Engineers for a Sustainable World, we are working to provide clean water at BOOA's educational center.

Hygiene

  • We are training the community on proper waste disposal and the importance of handwashing.

Future Projects

We are open to requests for assistance with the following areas:

Infrastructure Improvements

  • Wells, bathrooms, classrooms, energy.

Human Rights Trainings

  • Women’s rights, children’s rights, displaced persons’ rights.
