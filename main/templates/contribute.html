Partnerships & Supporting Institutions

IMPACT is a Beninese organization that focuses on agricultural and health projects.
Rumie is a registered nonprofit in both the USA and Canada, and it supplies affordable tablets for refugees. BOOA uses six Rumie tablets at the school.
One Million Lights is a Palo Alto based nonprofit that fundraises and distributes solar lights. BOOA’s light solicitation can be viewed here.
Cercle Social is a registered nonprofit in Texas that sponsors four high schools in Benin. CS is helping to connect BOOA students with higher education opportunities because our English first language students cannot attend French only public schools.
The UCLA School of Law sponsors Kathleen Bush-Joseph’s Public Service Law Fellowship with BOOA. We are developing opportunities for UCLA Law students to intern at BOOA the summer of 2017.
BOOA is formulating an intern placement plan for the summer of 2018 with Georgetown University’s Beeck Center for Social Impact and Innovation.
The Swiss Graduate Institute for International and Development Studies and its Afrique Club provide BOOA with faculty mentoring and fundraising assistance.
BOOA is exploring solar and sanitation projects with Northwestern University’s Engineers for a Sustainable World.
The Hinsdale Public Library in Illinois has donated children’s books to BOOA.
The LA South Bay Chapter of the Awesome Foundation awarded BOOA its first grant in November 2016.
